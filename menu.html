Our Menu

Appetizers

  • BBQ Pork Buns - Soft, fluffy buns filled with savory barbecued pork.
  • Spring Rolls (2 pcs) - Crispy rolls filled with julienne vegetables and glass noodles, served with a sweet chili dipping sauce.
  • Chili Oil Wontons (6 pcs) - Wontons filled with seasoned pork, drizzled with a spicy chili oil and garnished with green onions.
  • Dumplings (6 pcs) - Steamed or fried dumplings stuffed with your choice of pork or vegetables, accompanied by a soy and vinegar dip.

Main Courses

  • Ma Po Tofu - Soft tofu in a spicy, pungent bean sauce with minced pork.
  • Beef and Broccoli - Stir-fried beef slices with fresh broccoli in a rich garlic sauce.
  • Shrimp Fried Rice - Fried rice with shrimp, eggs, peas, carrots, and green onions.
  • Peking Duck - Roasted duck with crispy skin, served with pancakes, scallions, and hoisin sauce.
  • Szechuan Spicy Chicken - Deep-fried chicken tossed with a spicy Szechuan sauce, served with pickled vegetables.
  • Kung Pao Chicken - A Sichuan-inspired dish featuring tender chicken stir-fried with peanuts, vegetables, and chili peppers in a savory and slightly sweet sauce.

Desserts

  • Lychee Sorbet - A refreshing sorbet made from sweet lychee fruits.
  • Mango Pudding - A silky, mango-flavored dessert topped with fresh mango pieces and a light syrup.
  • Sesame Balls - Sweet dough balls coated with sesame seeds and filled with red bean paste, served hot.

Beverages

  • Lychee Juice - Sweet and refreshing juice made from fresh lychees.
  • Beer Tsingtao - Popular Chinese beer, perfect complement to spicy dishes.
  • Bubble Tea - Taiwanese tea-based drink that contains tea, flavored milk, and tapioca pearls.
  • Chinese Tea - A selection of traditional Chinese teas including Jasmine, Oolong, and Green tea.
